The assignment

You are an Arbiter. By the time you drop in, the world beneath you has already been condemned. You are there to carry out the sentence.

ARCDROP is a single-player, third-person shooter roguelite set in a fracturing sci-fi universe. Unstable realities called Arc Dimensions keep splintering off the Prime Dimension, growing and mutating until they threaten to pull everything under.

Drop in, deploy your Shell, and collapse them completely. There is no exploration prize and no world worth keeping. Every run is a wager: how much you carry, how far past safety you push, and what you're willing to lose when it goes wrong.

Field systems

Every run is a bet you place with your own gear.

Four systems shape every drop. How you play them decides the run.

SYS_01

The SHELL

Your deployable enclave and your lifeline. Anchor it at a Gravity Well and its buffs radiate through a fixed boundary, so dying out in the field costs you almost nothing. Wear it on your back instead and you carry every buff into the deep zone, with everything you own riding on staying alive.

SYS_02

The Pod Array

Slot Pods into your Shell to reshape a run around sprint, stealth, traversal, salvage, or time control. Reverse-engineer what you find to learn it forever, then craft and re-spec your loadout between engagements. Recipes stay with you no matter how a run ends.

SYS_03

Flux & Supercharge

Hoard Flux in the field and it sits there as raw fuel. Invert it at a Field Inverter and it becomes over-health, a Supercharged spike that soaks incoming fire before your health takes a hit, while your reserve stays intact. The same Flux also pays for everything you craft.

SYS_04

The Boundary

Every buff dies at the edge of your Shell's boundary, and the best loot always sits well outside it. Go in light with little to lose, or bring the Shell and bring the risk along with it. The map itself is where the pressure comes from.

NLOC // Operative Briefing · 001 Classified

The Divergence Event

An unexplained fracture split reality into unstable offshoots. Left alone, these Arc Dimensions grow, mutate, and drag on the fabric of existence. The response is absolute. Every one of them gets collapsed.

The NLOC

The Nonlinear Operatives Command is a shadow division under an unnamed global initiative. Its mission is to stabilize the Prime Dimension by collapsing the offshoots, and it works in total secrecy.
